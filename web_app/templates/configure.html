💡 Tips & Best Practices

        

            

                
🎯 For Quick Testing

                

                    Use a narrower pH range (e.g., 5.0–6.0) and fewer temperatures (3–4 values) to get results in ~10–30 seconds.
                

            

            

                
📊 For Detailed Analysis

                

                    Use finer grids (pH step 0.2, Temp step 2) and longer simulation times (30–50 days) for publication-quality results.
                

            

            

                
💾 Save & Compare

                

                    Each run is saved independently. Go to History to compare different parameter sets side-by-side.
