💡 Analysis & Insights
🧬 Biological Basis

Low pH (5.0–5.5) favors acidogenic bacteria like Clostridium species that produce H₂ from glucose. Methanogens are inhibited below pH 6.0, preventing H₂ consumption.

🌡️ Temperature Effects

The 30°C optimum reflects a compromise: kinetics increase with temperature, but thermodynamic constraints and oxygen solubility limit the sweet spot. Mesophilic range (30–35°C) is preferred for dark fermentation.
